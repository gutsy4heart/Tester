1. I am taken from a mine and shut up in a wooden case, from which I am never released, and yet I am used by almost every person. What am I?
2. The more you take, the more you leave behind. What am I?
3. I speak without a mouth and hear without ears. I have no body, but I come alive with the wind. What am I?
4. What has keys but can't open locks?
5. I am always hungry. I must always be fed. The finger I lick will soon turn red. What am I?
6. The person who makes it, sells it. The person who buys it never uses it. What is it?
7. I am taken from a mine, and shut up in a wooden case, from which I am never released, and yet I am used by almost every person. What am I?
8. What comes once in a minute, twice in a moment, but never in a thousand years?
9.  I have keys but open no locks. I have space but no room. You can enter, but you can't go inside. What am I?
10. The more you take, the more you leave behind. What am I?
11. What has an endless supply of letters but starts empty?
12. The person who builds it, sells it. The person who buys it never uses it. What is it?
